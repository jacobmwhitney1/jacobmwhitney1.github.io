A common sense approach to learning!

Each tutoring session is focused on meeting the student at his or her level, building problem solving skills, and developing a growth mindset that supports their personal goals.

In five years as an educator, my objective is to emulate the professors and studymates who helped me as a student. Math was not always my strong suit, and those who taught clearly, step-by-step and had fun with topics, stood out among the rest. Using these methods helps students to see math as a tool, instead of a barrier, for what they want to achieve.

Message me with any questions or to schedule a lesson.

Tutoring Qualifications

My tutor experience started by working with family and friends, covering multiple subjects and preparing me to be quick on my feet and prepared for any topic. Professionaly, I have worked in education for the past five years, first at Valencia College as a student leader. Here I guided study groups in and out of the classroom, covering college level math, study skills and test preparation. On the recommendation of a coworker, I began tutoring individually and refining my skills to better equip students for testing and real world problem solving. My education background includes an Associate's degree from Valencia College and credits from FSU towards a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.
